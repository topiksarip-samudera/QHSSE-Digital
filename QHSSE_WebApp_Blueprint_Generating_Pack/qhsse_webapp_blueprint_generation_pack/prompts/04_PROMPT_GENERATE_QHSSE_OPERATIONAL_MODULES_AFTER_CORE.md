# Prompt Generate QHSSE Operational Modules Setelah Core Selesai

Setelah Phase 1, Phase 2, dan Phase 3 core selesai, lanjutkan modul operasional QHSSE secara sequence:

1. Risk Management / HIRADC / JSA
2. Incident Management / Investigation / RCA / CAPA
3. Audit & Inspection
4. Permit to Work
5. Document Control
6. Training & Competency
7. Legal & Compliance Register
8. Environment Management
9. Quality Management
10. Security Management
11. Emergency Response
12. Contractor Management
13. Asset & Equipment

Gunakan core platform yang sudah dibuat:
- workflow
- form builder
- checklist builder
- action tracking
- notification
- attachment
- audit log
- role/permission
- module ON/OFF
- numbering
- dashboard
