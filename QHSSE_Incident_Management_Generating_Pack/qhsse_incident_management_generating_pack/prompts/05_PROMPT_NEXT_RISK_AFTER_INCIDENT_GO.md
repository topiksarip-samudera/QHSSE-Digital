# Prompt Next Module After Incident GO

Incident Management sudah lulus:

`INCIDENT MANAGEMENT STABILIZED: GO`

Sekarang lanjut ke QHSSE Operational Module berikutnya:

`Risk Management / HIRADC / JSA`

Aturan:
- Jangan mulai Risk sebelum Incident GO.
- Gunakan integrasi dari Incident:
  - Incident triggers risk review
  - High severity incident links to risk register
  - Lessons learned can create risk control
- Bangun Risk Management dengan sequence terpisah.
